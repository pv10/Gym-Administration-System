Our Vision

In 1993, the Stay Strong Gym opened in Bournemouth in the South Of England. Over the next decade and a half, Stay Strong was present in over 16 countries, including India making it a leader in the global fitness industry. In 2018, Stay Strong India was acquired by cure.fit, India's largest chain of fitness centres that offers energetic group workouts and multiple workout formats to choose from.
